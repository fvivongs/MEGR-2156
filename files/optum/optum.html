Healthcare and Technology. The perfect match

Optum is a technology development firm that operates under United Health Group. The company realizes that technology has evolved exponentially over the years, however, healthcare has not. Their mission is to meet current healthcare problems with innovative solutions and a focus around user experience.

Setting the path for future innovations

With 8 members, our team developed an application to improve the way doctors interact with patient information on EHR systems. Our product concept name is "Envision", a FHIR Visualization software that works as a plug-in application on top of any EHR system. All of this was developed at Optum TDP which works in agile; a system made to make tasks and communication flexible opposed to traditional workflow methods.

Making a difference

It was never about sitting behind a laptop and developing an application that consumers could use. I got the opportunity to talk to real clients and professionals in their field to discuss problems and how they could be met with impactful solutions. Along the way I sharpened my skills in communication, presenting, project management, and even learned how to program. Working at this company sparked my interest in software development and motivated me to learn more about the skills which allowed me to code this very website.

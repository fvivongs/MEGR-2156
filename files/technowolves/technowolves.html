The start of TechnoWolves. A new beginning...

The team was founded in 2014 by my colleagues and I to promote STEM at our school and in our community. The organization gives high school students the opportunity to learn about business and engineering through an international competition provided by the organization For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology (FIRST).

The evolution of our team.

The robotics club quickly sparked interest in many students at our school and by its second year gained over 50 participants. Thanks to the support of the parents, community, and sponsors; the team currently has its own workshop based in Apex, NC.

Volunteering and Outreach. The community loves robots!

This organization isn’t just a robotics club, it’s a business. At Techno Wolves we teach students how to pitch to companies, manage team finances, and give back to the community through outreach activities and volunteer events.

TechnoWolves Victory Leading the Pack

With over 50 student members, in addition to mentors and parent volunteers, the Techno Wolves family continues to grow since its creation. New acheievments are in the making and the team's notoriety within the community continues to expand as new generations pick up the responsibilities left behind by graduating students.

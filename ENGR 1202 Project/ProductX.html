Our Product

Toothy is a compact travel container that safely carries your oral hygiene necessities and provides a convenient bin when not stored in your luggage or pocket. It is designed to prevent from opening if it happens to tumble inside your luggage. Being waterproof inside and out avoids any remaining moisture from permeating through or leaking onto surrounding items or clothing. This also prevents any outside liquids or dirt from getting inside the case as well.

Our innovative design allows you to comfortably fit your oral necessities into most standard deep pockets which allows you to freshen up anytime and anywhere without the bulk.

What is Project Toothy?

Project Toothy is our team's solution for the ENGR 1202 project. Students are assigned the task of developing a fully concealed container that holds any toiletry objects of their choice. Toothy is a oral hygiene product that contains all your basic teeth needs for “on the go” situations!
